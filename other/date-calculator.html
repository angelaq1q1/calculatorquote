📅 Date & Time Calculation FAQ: Periods, Age, and Excel Formulas

Q1: How can you calculate your due date?
A: You can calculate your pregnancy due date by counting 40 weeks (280 days) from the first day of your last menstrual period. There are also online pregnancy calculators that can do this automatically.

Q2: How to calculate your next period date?
A: To estimate your next period, add your average menstrual cycle length (usually 28 days) to the first day of your last period. This gives you an approximate date for your next cycle.

Q3: How to calculate the difference in dates in Excel?
A: In Excel, you can calculate the difference between two dates using the formula: =End_Date - Start_Date. This will return the number of days between the dates.

Q4: How to calculate age in Excel with date of birth?
A: Use the formula =DATEDIF(Birth_Date, TODAY(), "Y") in Excel. It will calculate the number of full years from the birth date to today.

Q5: How can I calculate my due date of pregnancy?
A: You can calculate your due date by adding 280 days (or 40 weeks) to the first day of your last menstrual period. Many online calculators can provide this date instantly.

Q6: How to tell the date in Blue Prince?
A: You need to add the number of days you have been exploring (shown on the pause screen) to that start date to figure out the date in Blue Prince. For example, if you are on "Day 22" of exploration, then the current date in the game would be Nov. 28.
