Q2: How to calculate the cost of plumbing?

A: Plumbers charge $45 to $150 per hour with a minimum service call-out fee of $50 to $200. Small plumbing repairs like unclogging a toilet or fixing a leaky faucet typically cost $125 to $350. Larger jobs such as repairing the plumbing on a pipe leak, you'll pay about $500 to $800.

Q3: What is a plumbing waste stack?

A: The plumbing stack is a long pipe that runs vertically from the top to the bottom of the home. All drains in the building connect to the plumbing stack, which connects in turn to the horizontal sewer lateral outside of the home. That carries waste out to the municipal sewer main.
