Q: How much do solar panels cost for a 2,000 sq ft house?
A: For a 2,000 sq ft home, solar panel installation typically costs between $15,000 to $25,000 before incentives. After the 30% federal tax credit, your net cost would be $10,500 to $17,500. Use our solar panel cost calculator with your specific location and energy usage for the most accurate estimate.

Q: What is the average cost per watt for solar panels?
A: The average cost per watt for solar panels in 2024 ranges from $2.50 to $3.50, depending on system size, location, and equipment quality. Larger systems typically cost less per watt due to economies of scale. Our calculator uses current market rates for accurate estimates.

Q: How long does it take to pay off solar panels?
A: Solar panel payback periods typically range from 6 to 12 years, depending on your electricity rates, system cost, and available incentives. In states with high electricity rates and good incentives, payback can be as short as 4-6 years. Use our calculator to see your specific payback timeline.

Q: Do solar panels increase home value?
A: Yes, solar panels typically increase home value by 3-4% on average. According to studies, homes with solar panels sell for about $15,000 more than comparable homes without solar. The exact value increase depends on your location, system size, and local real estate market.

Q: What happens to solar panels during power outages?
A: Standard grid-tied solar systems automatically shut off during power outages for safety reasons. However, systems with battery backup (like Tesla Powerwall) can continue providing power to your home during outages. Battery storage adds $10,000-$15,000 to your total system cost.

Q: How do I get the best solar panel installation quote?
A: Get quotes from at least 3-5 local solar installers, compare equipment quality and warranties, check installer reviews and certifications, and ask about financing options. Use our solar panel cost calculator first to understand your system requirements, then use that information to evaluate quotes effectively.
