Q1: How much is pet grooming at PetSmart?
A: PetSmart grooming prices typically range from $30-80 for basic services, with full grooming packages costing $50-120 depending on pet size and services. Our calculator provides estimates that are competitive with PetSmart's pricing structure, helping you compare costs across different grooming providers.

Q2: Why do different pet sizes cost different amounts?
A: Larger pets require more time, products, and effort. A Great Dane's bath uses significantly more shampoo than a Chihuahua's, and the grooming time scales accordingly.

Q3: What's included in "Full Grooming"?
A: Full grooming typically includes bath, blow-dry, haircut/trim, nail trimming, ear cleaning, and basic styling. It's the complete spa package for your pet.

Q4: Is home grooming service worth the extra cost?
A: Home service is great for anxious pets, busy schedules, or multiple pets. The convenience often outweighs the extra cost, especially if you have multiple pets or a stressed-out fur baby.

Q5: How often should I groom my pet?
A: It depends on breed, fur type, and lifestyle. Most dogs need grooming every 4-8 weeks, while cats may need it every 6-12 weeks. Long-haired breeds typically need more frequent grooming.
