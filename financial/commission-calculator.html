How This Calculator Works

Using this commission calculator is easy. Enter the total sale amount and your commission rate. The calculator works for fixed percentages or tiered rates.

Example: Tiered Commission for Annual Sales

Suppose your yearly sales total is $50,000. Your company uses a tiered commission structure:

Sales Range Commission Rate Calculation
$0 – $10,000 5% $10,000 × 5% = $500
$10,001 – $30,000 7% $20,000 × 7% = $1,400
$30,001 – $50,000 10% $20,000 × 10% = $2,000

Total commission = $500 + $1,400 + $2,000 = $3,900

Using the calculator, you just enter the total sales and define your tiered rates. The tool instantly shows your total sales earnings, no manual math needed. This method works for any salesperson, freelancer, or agent whose commission rate changes with the sale amount.

Q1: What do you mean by sales commission?

A: A sales commission is the additional income a salesperson earns based on the number of sales they've made. Salespeople typically earn a base salary plus commission.

Q2: What is a typical sales commission?

A: The industry average for sales commission typically falls between 20% and 30% of gross margins. At the low end, sales professionals may earn 5% of a sale, while straight commission structures allow a 100% commission.
