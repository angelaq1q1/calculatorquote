Q2: How does compound interest work?

A: Compound interest works by adding earned interest back into your balance so it can earn interest again. This creates a "snowball effect," where money grows faster over time compared to simple interest.

Q3: How to invest compound interest?

A: You can invest with compound interest through savings accounts, CDs, bonds, or reinvesting dividends in stocks. The key is consistency and timeâ€”regular contributions allow compound interest to work more effectively.
