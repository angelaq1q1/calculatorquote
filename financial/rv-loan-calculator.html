About RV Loans

What is an RV Loan?

An RV loan is a type of financing specifically designed for purchasing recreational vehicles, including motorhomes, travel trailers, fifth wheels, and campers. These loans typically have longer terms than auto loans, ranging from 5 to 20 years, and may have different interest rates and qualification requirements.

How RV Loan Calculations Work

Our RV loan calculator uses the standard loan amortization formula:

M = P × r × (1 + r)ⁿ / ((1 + r)ⁿ - 1)

Where:

  • M = Monthly payment
  • P = Principal loan amount (RV price - down payment)
  • r = Monthly interest rate (annual rate ÷ 12)
  • n = Total number of payments (years × 12)

RV Loan Terms and Considerations

  • Loan Terms: Typically 5-20 years, longer than auto loans
  • Interest Rates: May be higher than home loans but lower than personal loans
  • Down Payment: Usually 10-20% of the RV price
  • Credit Requirements: Generally require good to excellent credit
  • Insurance: RV insurance is typically required

Benefits of Early Payments

Making extra payments on your RV loan can significantly reduce the total interest paid and shorten the loan term. Even small additional payments can make a big difference over the life of the loan.

Tips for RV Financing

  • Shop around for the best interest rates and terms
  • Consider the total cost of ownership, including insurance, maintenance, and storage
  • Make a larger down payment if possible to reduce monthly payments
  • Factor in additional costs like fuel, campground fees, and maintenance
  • Consider the resale value and depreciation of the RV

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What are the current RV loan interest rates?

Current RV loan interest rates typically range between 4.5% and 8.5%, depending on lender, loan term, and creditworthiness.

How much is the RV loan percentage rate for new vs used RVs?

New RVs generally qualify for lower loan percentage rates (around 5% to 7%), while used RVs often carry higher rates (6% to 11%) due to increased lending risk.

What is the RV loan rate at USAA?

USAA offers competitive RV loan rates usually ranging from 4.75% to 7.5%, varying based on membership and credit profile.

How do I calculate RV loan payments with a down payment?

To calculate payments with a down payment, subtract the down payment from the total loan amount, then apply your loan's interest rate and term to compute the monthly payment.
