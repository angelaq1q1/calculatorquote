Q1: What is this income tax?

Q2: What income is not taxable?

A: Nontaxable income won't be taxed, whether or not you enter it on your tax return. The following items are deemed nontaxable by the IRS: inheritances, gifts and bequests. cash rebates on items you purchase from a retailer, manufacturer or dealer.

Q3: Do I pay tax on income?

A: Most income is subject to income tax, including income from employment, self-employment, private and state pensions, investments and property rental.

Q4: How to solve income tax?

A: What is the basic formula for calculating taxable income? The formula is your Gross Income (total compensation and other income) minus allowable Deductions and Exemptions. The resulting Net Taxable Income is then used to calculate the tax due based on the official progressive tax brackets.
