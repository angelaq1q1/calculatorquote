❓ FAQ – Your Questions, Our No-BS Answers

Q: Why are there different ideal weight formulas?
A: Because science isn't one-size-fits-all. Different formulas were developed for different populations and purposes. We show you multiple ranges so you can get a better picture.

Q: Is this the same as BMI?
A: Nope! While BMI is a quick health indicator, ideal weight formulas are more specific and take into account your frame size and gender.

Q: What if my weight is outside the ideal range?
A: Don't panic! These are guidelines, not gospel. Talk to your doctor about what's healthy for your specific body and lifestyle.

Q: Do I need to be exactly at my ideal weight?
A: No way! A healthy weight is a range, not a single number. Focus on how you feel and your overall health, not just the scale.

🧠 Pro tip:

Remember: Your ideal weight is just one piece of the health puzzle. Focus on building healthy habits, staying active, and eating well. The number on the scale is just a number — it doesn't define your worth or health.
