FAQ: Negotiating Medical Bills

Q1: Is it legal to negotiate hospital bills?
A: Yes. You have every right to ask questions, request itemized charges, and request discounts or financial aid.

Q2: What if I already paid part of the bill?
A: You can still request a review or ask for retroactive assistance. Don't assume it's too late.

Q3: Will negotiating hurt my credit?
A: Not if you act quickly. Bills typically don't hit your credit report until 180 days after being sent to collections.

Q4: Can I negotiate even if I have insurance?
A: Yes. Especially if you're dealing with out-of-network charges or high deductibles.

Q5: Are there free resources?
A: Yes! Try nonprofit groups like the Patient Advocate Foundation or contact your state's consumer protection office.
