How the Calculator Works and Why It Matters

The calculator compares your waist and hip measurements to find a ratio. A good waist hip ratio for women is usually below 0.85, while for men it is below 0.90. If your number is higher, it may mean there is too much fat around your waist. Doctors say that extra belly fat increases the risk of heart disease, diabetes, and other health problems.

To use the calculator, measure your waist at the narrowest part and your hips at the widest part. Then divide your waist size by your hip size. For example, if your waist is 30 inches and your hips are 40 inches, your waist to hip ratio is 0.75. That is considered a healthy or hourglass waist to hip ratio.

If your result is a 0.6 waist to hip ratio, your body is in great balance, and your risk level is lower. But if your ratio is higher than normal, it may be time to make a few changes ‚Äî like eating better, moving more, and managing stress. This ratio is not about looks. Tracking your ratio over time is also a good idea. Even small changes, such as losing one inch from your waist or building stronger hips, can make a real difference. The calculator helps you see your progress clearly and stay motivated.
