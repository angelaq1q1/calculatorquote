Q1. Are there benefits to ingesting fluoride?

A: Drinking fluoridated water keeps teeth strong and reduces cavities by about 25% in children and adults. This results in less mouth pain, fewer fillings or teeth pulled, and fewer missed days of work and school.

Q2. Is it safe to eat fluoride?

A: While large amounts of fluoride can be dangerous, it's very difficult to reach toxic levels with properly fluoridated water and over-the-counter products that contain fluoride. Talk to your dentist to learn more and find out if you need professional fluoride treatments.
