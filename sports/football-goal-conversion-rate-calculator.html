Q1: What is goal conversion rate in soccer?
A: Goal conversion rate in soccer measures how efficiently a player or team converts their shots into goals. It's calculated by dividing the number of goals scored by the total number of shots taken, then multiplying by 100 to get a percentage. This metric helps players and coaches understand shooting efficiency and identify areas for improvement.
