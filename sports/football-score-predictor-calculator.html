Looking to predict high school football match results using real data? 📝 How to Fill in Attack/Defense Rating?

  • Based on Data (Recommended):
    Attack Rating: (Team Avg Goals ÷ League Avg Goals) × 2, limit to 1~3.
    Defense Rating: (League Avg Conceded ÷ Team Avg Conceded) × 2, limit to 1~3.
    Example: If a team scores 2.0 per game, league avg is 1.5, then Attack Rating = (2.0/1.5)×2 ≈ 2.67.
    If a team concedes 0.8 per game, league avg is 1.2, then Defense Rating = (1.2/0.8)×2 = 3 (max 3).
  • Subjective Estimate:
    If no data, rate 1 (weak), 2 (average), 3 (strong) based on your impression.
  • Reference Third-Party Data:
    Use ratings from sites like FiveThirtyEight SPI, WhoScored, SofaScore, or official league stats.

Tip: Attack = higher is better; Defense = higher means stronger defense (fewer goals conceded).
