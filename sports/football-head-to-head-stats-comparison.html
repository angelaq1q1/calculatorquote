Football Head-to-Head Stats Comparison

Quickly compare two soccer teams based on their head-to-head matches, match history, and key performance stats. Our football match statistics comparison tool is perfect for fans, analysts, and prediction enthusiasts looking to analyze head-to-head football games.

📝 How to Fill Each Field & Calculation Logic

  • Team A Name / Team B Name: Enter the names of the two teams you want to compare (e.g., Chelsea, PSG). Used for display in the table.
  • Matches Played: Total number of head-to-head matches between the two teams (e.g., 18). Used for average goals and win rate calculations.
  • Team A Wins / Team B Wins / Draws: Number of matches each team won, and number of draws. Used for W/D/L distribution.
  • Team A Goals / Team B Goals: Total goals scored by each team in all head-to-head matches. Used for total and average goals.
  • Team A Home (W D L G) / Team A Away (W D L G): Enter A team's home/away wins, draws, losses, and goals (e.g., "5 2 2 13"). Shows home/away performance.
  • Team B Home (W D L G) / Team B Away (W D L G): Enter B team's home/away wins, draws, losses, and goals (e.g., "4 2 3 14").
  • Recent Trend: Summary of the last 5 head-to-head results (e.g., "Team B last 5: 3W 1D 1L").
  • Important Matches: Head-to-head record in important matches (e.g., finals, "Champions League H2H: Team B 2W 1L").

Calculation logic: The tool uses your input to display win/draw/loss distribution, total and average goals, home/away performance, and recent/important match summaries. All stats are based on your entries.
